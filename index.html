Journey at a Glance 

A chronology of leadership, public service, and national administration.

1964

Birth & Values

Born in Mumbai, raised with traditional values and early study of Indian scriptures.

1980

RSS & Grassroots

Joined RSS as a swayamsevak; started work as a student leader and booth worker.

1997

Elected MLA

Elected to the Gujarat Legislative Assembly, representing Sarkhej with record margins.

2014

National President

Appointed as BJP National President; expanded the party's footprint to every corner of India.

2019

Union Home Minister

Assumed office as Union Home Minister; guided major security and legislative initiatives.

2021

Ministry of Cooperation

Assumed the newly created portfolio to strengthen the cooperative movement in India.

2024

Historic Reforms

Implemented modern criminal justice codes and landmark development projects nationwide.
